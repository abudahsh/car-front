DEALER POLICY


How We Use and Protect Your Customer Data

BAIC does not, nor will we ever, share or sell your Sales Matching data to any Dealer, OEM or third-party. Your Dealer Sales Matching data is kept confidential and secure. Data from unmatched sales, meaning sales you made to consumers who did not use BAIC, is erased within 90 days. A Big Four accounting firm has tested and validated our policy of erasing data from unmatched sales within 90 days. To see the report, please contact your BAIC representative or email DataPolicy@baic.com.



Frequently Asked Questions


Below are answers to some typical questions that we hear from Dealers.



Why does TrueCar require Sales Matching data from participating Dealers?


At TrueCar, we believe that you should only pay for the value that you receive from our service. In an effort to provide a fully accountable business model, we offer a Pay Per Sale billing model for Dealers in most states. Under this model, the list of customers who buy cars from you is matched with the registration information of TrueCar users who connected with you using one of our platforms. That way, you are only charged when you sell a car to one of our users.



I am a Dealer operating on a subscription billing model. Why do I need to provide TrueCar with my Sales Matching Data?


For dealerships on this model, we still report matched sales to help Dealers measure the value they are receiving from our service. In some states, the matched sales data is also used as a factor in determining your accountable subscription rate, which is set in advance to help you budget your expenses.



What is Sales Matching data NOT used for?


TrueCar will never share or sell your data to any Dealer, OEM or third-party. TrueCar does not use your Sales Matching data to contact or send marketing communications to any of your customers.



For how long does TrueCar retain Dealer Sales Matching data?

TrueCar only retains Dealer Sales Matching data for TrueCar consumers who bought a car from you as long as is required to comply with our tax, audit or legal obligations. Data from unmatched sales, meaning sales you made to consumers who did not use TrueCar, is erased within 90 days. As part of our Dealer Pledge, a Big Four accounting firm has tested and validated our policy of erasing data from unmatched sales within 90 days. To see the report, please contact your TrueCar representative or email DataPolicy@truecar.com.



How does TrueCar access my DMS data?


TrueCar does not have direct access to Dealer DMS systems. Sales Matching data (for all Dealers) and Transaction Pricing data (for Dealers who opt-in) is transmitted to TrueCar by DMS providers or is collected and transmitted to TrueCar by other Dealer approved third-parties.



How does TrueCar protect Dealer DMS data?


Protecting Dealer DMS data is of utmost importance to TrueCar. TrueCar employs a multi-layered approach that incorporates AES encryption, network isolation, and secure data centers. Our dedicated security team performs audits of our operations throughout the year for our own purposes as well as to satisfy the requirements of the many companies who partner with TrueCar, including USAA.



Are TrueCar Certified Dealers required to provide Transaction Pricing data?


No. Participating Dealers are not required to share Transaction Pricing data. However, Dealers may choose to share Transaction Pricing data with TrueCar in order to obtain access to our Analytics Service, called Sales Analyzer, which contains advanced tools that can help Dealers optimize pricing strategies, reveal price/distance trade-offs and more. Transaction Pricing data is also used to populate and further improve the accuracy of our products and services, including the TrueCar Price Curve. This results in more accurate local pricing, benefiting Dealers and consumers alike.
